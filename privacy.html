Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 3, 2026

Overview

My Fasting Timer is designed with your privacy as a priority. The app does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All your information stays on your device.

Data Storage

All data — including fasting history, streaks, settings, and weight entries — is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data is not accessible to us or any third party.

No Data Collection

My Fasting Timer does not:

  • Collect personal information
  • Use analytics or tracking SDKs
  • Use advertising identifiers (IDFA)
  • Send data to external servers
  • Use crash reporting services
  • Create user accounts or require sign-in

Apple HealthKit

If you choose to enable the Apple Health integration (a Pro feature), the app reads weight data from the Health app to display trends. My Fasting Timer:

  • Only reads weight data — it never writes to HealthKit
  • Never transmits HealthKit data off your device
  • Never shares HealthKit data with third parties
  • Never uses HealthKit data for advertising or marketing

You can revoke HealthKit access at any time in Settings > Privacy & Security > Health.

In-App Purchases

Purchase transactions are processed entirely by Apple through StoreKit. We do not receive or store your payment information.

Children's Privacy

My Fasting Timer does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app does not require any personal information to function.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, we will post the revised version on this page with an updated date.
